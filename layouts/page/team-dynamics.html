Team Dynamics

Cohesion

Interdisciplinary teams work better together when they utilize a common process. The united Care Management Plan provides cohesion for the team.

Confidence

It is important for care providers to know that they are making a difference; that they are doing the best they can. POMMe-Health eliminates gaps in care.

Clarity

POMMe-Health reduces confusion for complex cases by delivering context for patient care. A common vision for the team for the patient is crucial for success.

Collaboration

POMMe-Health allows for true collaboration ... a better outcome with all working together, not in silos.

Transparency

Care providers know where they fit in and what they are responsible for. Knowing how each care provider's role fits into the care plan for the patient.
